Welcome to the World of BTS: A Global Phenomenon

BTS, also known as Bangtan Sonyeondan (Bulletproof Boy Scouts), is a South Korean boy band that has redefined the global music landscape. Since their debut in 2013, the seven-member group—RM, Jin, Suga, J-Hope, Jimin, V, and Jungkook—has captivated millions with their powerful performances, meaningful lyrics, and unique blend of genres.What started as a modest project under Big Hit Entertainment has blossomed into one of the most influential acts in music history. BTS is known for their messages of self-love, mental health awareness, and social change, using their platform to inspire fans around the world, known as ARMY. With record-breaking albums, sold-out tours, and a growing global presence, BTS has become more than just a band—they're a cultural force.Join us as we explore the music, the stories, and the impact of BTS—a group that continues to change the world, one song at a time.

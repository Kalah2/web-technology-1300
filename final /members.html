RM (Kim Nam-joon)

  • Born: September 12, 1994
  • Position: Leader, Rapper
  • Known for: RM, formerly known as Rap Monster, is the leader of BTS and one of the main rappers. He is fluent in English, which has helped BTS connect with their international fans. RM is known for his deep voice, intelligent lyrics, and philosophical reflections, often addressing topics such as self-discovery, life, and social issues. As a skilled songwriter and producer, RM has contributed significantly to BTS's music, and his leadership style is respected by both the members and their fans.
  • Fun Fact: RM was a part of an underground rap scene before joining Big Hit Entertainment, and he’s often praised for his impressive rap flow and ability to freestyle.

Jin (Kim Seok-jin)

  • Born: December 4, 1992
  • Position: Vocalist, Visual
  • Known for: Jin, the oldest member of BTS, is often referred to as the “visual” of the group due to his striking looks. He has a beautiful, melodious voice and is known for his emotional ballads, especially on songs like “Awake” and “Epiphany.” Despite being the oldest, Jin is playful and often brings humor and warmth to the group dynamic. He’s also known for his sense of responsibility as the eldest and often acts as a big brother figure to the younger members.
  • Fun Fact: Jin is known for his love of food and his “worldwide handsome” title, which he humorously gave himself, but his fans wholeheartedly agree!

Suga (Min Yoon-gi)

  • Born: March 9, 1993
  • Position: Rapper
  • Known for: Suga, also known as Agust D (his solo rap name), is one of the most prolific songwriters and producers in BTS. His rap style is characterized by its speed, sharp delivery, and emotional depth. He is very involved in the creation of BTS’s music and has produced and written a significant amount of their songs. Suga is known for his introspective lyrics, often dealing with themes of mental health, struggle, and personal growth. He’s also known for being a bit reserved, with a dry sense of humor and a calm demeanor.
  • Fun Fact: Suga’s mixtape, Agust D, received critical acclaim for its raw and personal content, and he’s also worked with numerous other artists as a producer.

J-Hope (Jung Ho-seok)

  • Born: February 18, 1994
  • Position: Dancer, Rapper
  • Known for: J-Hope is known for his exceptional dancing skills and positive energy. He’s the lead dancer of the group and brings incredible charisma to every performance. His rap style is known for being energetic and playful, often adding a burst of brightness to BTS’s music. J-Hope’s solo work, such as his mixtape Hope World, reflects his upbeat personality and creative style. He is the “ray of sunshine” in the group, always encouraging the members and fans with his infectious optimism.
  • Fun Fact: Before joining BTS, J-Hope was a street dancer and a member of the dance crew Neurotic. His background in dance has made him one of the best dancers in K-pop.

Jimin (Park Ji-min)

  • Born: October 13, 1995
  • Position: Lead Dancer, Vocalist
  • Known for: Jimin is famous for his stunning dance performances and angelic vocals. As the lead dancer, his smooth, graceful movements on stage make him stand out in every BTS performance. His voice is soft yet powerful, with a unique tone that can convey deep emotion. Jimin is also known for his kind-hearted, sensitive personality and his strong bond with the other members, particularly with Jungkook. His songs, such as “Serendipity” and “Filter,” showcase his beautiful voice and personal style.
  • Fun Fact: Jimin was originally trained as a contemporary dancer before joining BTS, which contributes to his elegant, fluid dancing style.

V (Kim Tae-hyung)

  • born: December 30, 1995
  • position: Vocalist
  • Known for: V is known for his deep, soulful voice, which adds a rich, distinct layer to BTS's music. His voice has a unique timbre that’s instantly recognizable, and he often sings the more emotional parts of their songs. V is also known for his quirky personality, creativity, and sense of humor. He is a visual standout, known for his striking features and charismatic presence. V’s solo songs like “Singularity” and “Inner Child” have shown his versatility as an artist, both vocally and emotionally.
  • Fun Fact: V is an avid fan of photography and even has a passion for taking photos, often sharing his artistic shots with fans.

Jungkook (Jeon Jung-kook)

  • born: September 1, 1997
  • position: Main Vocalist, Lead Dancer, Maknae (youngest)
  • Known for: Jungkook is often referred to as the “Golden Maknae” because he excels in nearly every area: singing, dancing, and even songwriting. As the youngest member, Jungkook is often seen as the group’s powerhouse vocalist and dancer. His talent, both as a performer and an artist, has earned him a reputation for being versatile and hardworking. Jungkook has released several solo tracks, like “Euphoria” and “My Time,” which showcase his vocal ability and emotional range.
  • Fun Fact: Despite being the youngest, Jungkook often takes on leadership roles in performances and is known for his strong work ethic and determination.
